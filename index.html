Top three activities to do at Lagos

Buy Suya at night

There are many roadway stations set up at night that sell the cultural delicacy called Suya. It is different grilled meat peppered and prepared using yaji, a local spice

Visit the Lekki Conservation Centre

There are many roadway stations set up at night that sell the cultural delicacy called Suya. It is different grilled meat peppered and prepared using yaji, a local spice

Visit Elegushi beach

Lagos is bordered by the Atlantic ocean on the south. A good way to spend your afternoon is on Elegushi beach, reclining and catching the warm sum.
